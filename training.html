Training Programs

KTechno has prepared several training programs for Bumiputera citizen.

Yayasan Peneraju Pendidikan Bumiputera

Yayasan Peneraju Pendidikan Bumiputera (Yayasan Peneraju) is an agency under the Prime Minister's Department (Special Duties) that focuses on strengthening capacity building towards sustainable Bumiputera talent.

Among the objectives of the Peneraju Foundation is to increase the quality, quantity and relevance of Bumiputera talent in line with the government's efforts to drive Malaysia towards a high-income country through academic funding programs, TVET certification, professionals, technology and specialists.

Therefore, Kulim Advanced Technologies Sdn. Bhd. has been selected by Yayasan Peneraju Pendidikan Bumiputera as a trainer programme to implement the Program Skil Iltizam and Program Skim Profesional.

PROGRAM SKIL ILTIZAM

The Peneraju Skil Iltizam Program is to give a second chance for student (primary/secondary/college) or any individual aged 18-25 years old that acquire interest in technical skills compare to academic. Thru this program, the student will become certified skilled person according to industry’s requirements and job placement.

Kulim Advanced Technologies Sdn. Bhd. is a certified training center for the Yayasan Peneraju and there are several courses offered by Peneraju Skil Iltizam for those of you who are interested in joining.

Among the courses provided through this program are:
(For those interested, please click the course below)

⦿ CNC Machining Technician
⦿ Quality Control
⦿ Metal Fabrication
⦿ Industrial Automation & Robotics

PROGRAM PENJANA PERKESO

What is the Penjana Kerjaya 2.0 ? Program Insentif Pengambilan Pekerja, which was previously in place, has been replaced by Penjana Perkeso 2.0. Beginning 1 January 2021, Penjana Kerjaya 2.0 will accept an applications.

As of 24 February 2021, Kulim Advanced Technologies has been recognized as a training provider under Penjana Kerjaya Perkeso 2.0. Employers who want to conduct training with us under the programme are welcome in Ktechno.

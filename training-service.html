Training Services Certificates

Training Services Certificates

Our training department had been established to provide training needs from various walks of life. To date, we had trained more than 1000 participants throughout Malaysia.

From Vendor Development Program then Vendor & Entrepreneur Development Program, Skills Upgrading Program for Small & Medium Enterprises with SMECorp, Architectural Drawing with CIDB , followed by Finishing School, Graduates Employability Program with MOHE and lately with Yayasan Peneraju Pendidikan Bumiputera, we had vast experienced in managing and conducting training.

We are HRDF Certified Training Provider, Fanuc Certified Training Centre and in 2016 we received a certification from City & Guilds for 3528-08.

Our expertise areas are related to precision machining, robotics, sheet metal fabrication, quality control as well as other soft skill programs. To facilitate the training process, we had been equipped with simulators lab, robotics lab, computers lab and drafting studio.
